Child Safety Policy.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Commitment to Child Safety

crcle (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users.

We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (“CSAE”) and for any content or behavior that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers children.

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on crcle:

  • Child sexual abuse material (“CSAM”)
  • Grooming a child for sexual exploitation
  • Sextortion or coercion involving a child
  • Trafficking of a child for sexual purposes
  • Sexual messaging, solicitation, or predatory behavior involving minors
  • Any other content or conduct that exploits, abuses, or endangers children

3. Reporting Concerns

If you encounter content or behavior that may involve the exploitation or endangerment of a child, please report it through the app where that feature is available or contact us immediately by email.

Contact: info@crcleapp.com

4. Enforcement

We review reports and may take action including removing content, restricting features, suspending accounts, or permanently banning users who violate this policy.

5. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

Where appropriate, we may report suspected child sexual abuse or exploitation to law enforcement or relevant child protection authorities and cooperate with lawful investigations.

6. Age Requirements

crcle is not intended for children under the age of 13. Users must meet the minimum age required by applicable law to use the platform.

7. Ongoing Safety Measures

We may update our moderation, review, and safety practices over time to help prevent misuse of the platform and better protect our users.

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this Child Safety Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by the “Last Updated” date on this page.
